Wonders of Kyoto

Journey through time in Kyoto, the historical heart of Japan. Known for its preserved temples, sublime gardens, and traditional geisha districts, Kyoto provides a mesmerizing blend of ancient traditions and modern innovations.

List of Activities & Attractions:
  • Discover Kiyomizu-dera's panoramic views.
  • Tea ceremonies in Gion.
  • Walkthrough Arashiyama's Bamboo Forest.
  • Relish the autumn colors at Tofuku-ji Temple.
  • Experience a stay in a traditional ryokan.
